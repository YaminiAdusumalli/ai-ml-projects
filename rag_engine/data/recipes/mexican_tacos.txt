Dish Name: Mexican Chicken Tacos
Cuisine: Mexican

Ingredients:
- 6 taco shells
- 200g chicken, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Cook chicken with paprika, cumin and salt.
2. Warm taco shells in a pan.
3. Fill shells with chicken and onions.

Cooking Time: 15 minutes
Difficulty: Easy

Authentic Tips:
- Add lime juice and fresh cilantro before serving.
