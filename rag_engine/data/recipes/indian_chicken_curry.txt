Dish Name: Indian Chicken Curry
Cuisine: Indian

Ingredients:
- 500g chicken
- 2 onions, chopped
- 2 tomatoes, pureed
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1.5 tsp red chilli powder
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Heat oil and saut√© onions.
2. Add tomatoes and spices.
3. Add chicken and cook.
4. Add water and simmer until tender.

Cooking Time: 40 minutes
Difficulty: Medium

Authentic Tips:
- Use bone-in chicken for better flavour.
